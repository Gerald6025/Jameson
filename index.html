    About Us

    Jameson brings a light floral fragrance on the nose, peppered with spicy wood and sweet notes before an exceptional balance of spicy, nutty and vanilla tones on the palate with hints of sweet sherry that develop into an incredibly smooth and mellow finish.

    Jameson is known for its triple distillation process, which is a hallmark of Irish whiskey. This method involves distilling the spirit three times, resulting in a smoother and lighter flavor profile compared to many other whiskeys. The whiskey is made from a blend of malted and unmalted barley, which contributes to its unique taste. The barley is sourced from local farms, ensuring quality and sustainability.
